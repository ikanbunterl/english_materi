Tenses (Waktu)

Pelajari berbagai bentuk waktu dalam Bahasa Inggris

Simple Present Tense

Rumus:

Positive (+):

Subject + V1 (+s/es)

She plays tennis

Negative (-):

Subject + do/does + not + V1

She does not play tennis

Question (?):

Do/Does + Subject + V1?

Does she play tennis?

Contoh Kalimat:

+ I go to school every day

- I don't go to school on Sunday

? Do you go to school?

Penggunaan

1. Kebiasaan (Habits)

Untuk menyatakan kebiasaan yang dilakukan secara rutin

I drink coffee every morning

2. Fakta Umum (General Truth)

Untuk menyatakan fakta yang benar secara umum

The sun rises in the east

3. Kemampuan (Ability)

Untuk menyatakan kemampuan seseorang

She speaks English fluently

4. Jadwal (Schedule)

Untuk menyatakan jadwal yang tetap

The train leaves at 7 AM

Simple Past Tense

Rumus:

Positive (+):

Subject + V2

She played tennis

Negative (-):

Subject + did not + V1

She did not play tennis

Question (?):

Did + Subject + V1?

Did she play tennis?

Contoh Kalimat:

+ I went to the museum yesterday

- I didn't go to the party

? Did you visit Bali?

Penggunaan

1. Tindakan di Masa Lalu

Untuk menyatakan tindakan yang terjadi di masa lalu

I visited Japan last year

2. Rantai Tindakan

Untuk menyatakan beberapa tindakan yang terjadi berurutan

I woke up, took a shower, and went to school

3. Kebiasaan di Masa Lalu

Untuk menyatakan kebiasaan yang dilakukan di masa lalu

When I was young, I played football every day

4. Waktu Tertentu

Dengan keterangan waktu di masa lalu

Yesterday, I met my old friend

Past Continuous Tense

Rumus:

Positive (+):

Subject + was/were + V-ing

She was playing tennis

Negative (-):

Subject + was/were + not + V-ing

She was not playing tennis

Question (?):

Was/Were + Subject + V-ing?

Was she playing tennis?

Contoh Kalimat:

+ I was reading a book at 8 PM

- I wasn't studying last night

? Were you sleeping?

Penggunaan

1. Tindakan Berlangsung di Masa Lalu

Untuk menyatakan tindakan yang sedang berlangsung di masa lalu

I was reading when you called

2. Tindakan Paralel

Untuk menyatakan dua tindakan yang terjadi bersamaan

While I was studying, my brother was playing games

3. Latar Belakang Cerita

Untuk menggambarkan suasana atau latar dalam cerita

The sun was shining and birds were singing

4. Tindakan Terganggu

Untuk menyatakan tindakan yang terinterupsi

I was sleeping when the phone rang

Imperative Sentences

Kalimat perintah, permintaan, dan nasihat

Pengertian & Struktur

Imperative sentence adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk memberi perintah, permintaan, nasihat, atau instruksi. Subjek "you" biasanya tidak ditulis karena sudah dimengerti.

Struktur:

V1 (base form) + Object/Complement

Contoh: Close the door

Please + V1 + Object/Complement

Contoh: Please close the door

Do + V1 + Object/Complement

Contoh: Do close the door

Jenis & Contoh

1. Perintah (Command)

Sit down!

Open your book!

2. Permintaan (Request)

Please help me

Could you pass the salt?

3. Nasihat (Advice)

Be careful!

Don't forget your homework

4. Instruksi (Instruction)

Mix the flour and water

Turn left at the traffic light
