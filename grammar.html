Tenses (Waktu)

Pelajari berbagai bentuk waktu dalam Bahasa Inggris

Simple Present Tense

Rumus:

Positive (+):

Subject + V1 (+s/es)

She plays tennis

Negative (-):

Subject + do/does + not + V1

She does not play tennis

Question (?):

Do/Does + Subject + V1?

Does she play tennis?

Penggunaan:

  • • Kebiasaan - I go to school every day
  • • Fakta umum - The sun rises in the east
  • • Jadwal - The train leaves at 7 PM

Contoh Kalimat

Positive:

  • • I study English every night
  • • She works at a hospital
  • • They play football on weekends

Negative:

  • • I don't like coffee
  • • He doesn't work on Sundays
  • • We don't go to the cinema often

Question:

  • • Do you speak English?
  • • Does she live here?
  • • Do they work together?

Present Continuous Tense

Rumus:

Positive (+):

Subject + am/is/are + V-ing

She is playing tennis

Negative (-):

Subject + am/is/are + not + V-ing

She is not playing tennis

Question (?):

Am/Is/Are + Subject + V-ing?

Is she playing tennis?

Penggunaan:

  • • Sedang terjadi - I am studying now
  • • Rencana mendatang - I am meeting him tomorrow
  • • Perubahan - The climate is changing

Contoh Kalimat

Positive:

  • • I am reading a book now
  • • She is working at the moment
  • • They are playing football

Negative:

  • • I am not studying now
  • • He isn't working today
  • • We aren't playing games

Question:

  • • Are you studying English?
  • • Is she working now?
  • • Are they playing together?

Future Continuous Tense

Rumus:

Positive (+):

Negative (-):

Subject + will not be + V-ing

Question (?):

Will + Subject + be + V-ing?

Penggunaan:

  • • Sedang terjadi di masa depan - I will be studying at 8 PM tomorrow
  • • Rencana yang pasti - I will be meeting the CEO tomorrow
  • • Kejadian yang berlangsung - This time next week, I'll be flying to Paris

Contoh Kalimat

Positive:

  • • She will be working at this time tomorrow
  • • They will be playing football at 3 PM

Negative:

  • • I won't be studying tomorrow night
  • • She won't be working next weekend
  • • We won't be playing at that time

Question:

  • • Will you be studying tomorrow?
  • • Will she be working at 5 PM?
  • • Will they be playing football?
