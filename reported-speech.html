Reported Speech

Belajar cara melaporkan ucapan orang lain dengan benar

Apa itu Reported Speech?

Reported Speech (kalimat tidak langsung) adalah cara untuk melaporkan ucapan, perintah, atau pertanyaan orang lain tanpa mengutip kata-katanya secara langsung.

Direct vs Indirect Statements

Direct Speech (Kutipan Langsung)

Contoh: She said, "I am tired."

  • • Menggunakan tanda kutip (")
  • • Kata-kata asli dari pembicara
  • • Tense tetap sama

Indirect Speech (Kutipan Tidak Langsung)

Contoh: She said that she was tired.

  • • Tanpa tanda kutip
  • • Kata-kata dilaporkan
  • • Tense berubah
  • • Menggunakan kata penghubung "that"

Contoh Konversi

Present Simple → Past Simple

Direct: "I like coffee."

Indirect: She said (that) she liked coffee.

Present Continuous → Past Continuous

Direct: "I am studying English."

Indirect: He said (that) he was studying English.

Past Simple → Past Perfect

Direct: "I went to school."

Indirect: She said (that) she had gone to school.

Will → Would

Direct: "I will help you."

Indirect: He said (that) he would help me.

Reported Questions

Aturan Umum:

  • • Hilangkan tanda tanya (?) dan tanda kutip (")
  • • Ubah urutan kata menjadi kalimat berita
  • • Tambahkan kata tanya jika ada
  • • Ubah pronoun dan tense sesuai

Contoh Yes/No Questions:

Direct: "Are you tired?"

Indirect: She asked if/whether I was tired.

Direct: "Do you like coffee?"

Indirect: He asked if/whether I liked coffee.

Wh- Questions

What → asked what

Direct: "What are you doing?"

Indirect: She asked what I was doing.

Where → asked where

Direct: "Where do you live?"

Indirect: He asked where I lived.

When → asked when

Direct: "When will you arrive?"

Indirect: She asked when I would arrive.

Why → asked why

Direct: "Why are you late?"

Indirect: He asked why I was late.

How → asked how

Direct: "How do you know?"

Indirect: She asked how I knew.

Reported Commands

Struktur Umum:

Subject + told/asked/ordered + object + to + V1

  • • Positive command: told/asked + to + V1
  • • Negative command: told/asked + not + to + V1
  • • Pronoun berubah sesuai konteks

Contoh Positive Commands:

Direct: "Sit down, please."

Indirect: She asked me to sit down.

Direct: "Open the window."

Indirect: He told me to open the window.

Direct: "Be quiet!"

Indirect: The teacher ordered us to be quiet.

Negative Commands

Don't → asked/told not to

Direct: "Don't be late."

Indirect: She asked me not to be late.

Don't → told not to

Direct: "Don't touch that!"

Indirect: He told me not to touch that.

Never → asked never to

Direct: "Never give up."

Indirect: She asked me never to give up.

Daftar Kata Kerja Reporting:

Untuk Perintah:

  • • tell
  • • order
  • • command
  • • instruct

Untuk Permintaan:

  • • ask
  • • request
  • • beg
  • • urge

Perubahan Pronoun

Pronoun Changes:

I → he/she

Direct: "I am happy." → Indirect: She said she was happy.

You → I/he/she/they

Direct: "You are right." → Indirect: He said I was right.

My → his/her

Direct: "This is my book." → Indirect: She said it was her book.

Your → my/his/her/their

Direct: "I like your car." → Indirect: He said he liked my car.

Perubahan Keterangan Waktu & Tempat

Keterangan Waktu:

• now → then

• today → that day

• tomorrow → the next day

• yesterday → the day before

• this week → that week

• last month → the month before

• next year → the following year

Keterangan Tempat:

• here → there

• this → that

• these → those

Contoh:

Direct: "I will meet you here tomorrow."

Indirect: He said he would meet me there the next day.
