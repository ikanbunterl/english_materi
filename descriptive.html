Descriptive Text

Mempelajari cara menggambarkan orang, tempat, dan benda dengan bahasa yang menarik

Generic Structure

1

Identification

Bagian ini berfungsi untuk mengenalkan subjek yang akan dideskripsikan. Biasanya berisi informasi umum tentang siapa atau apa yang akan dibahas.

Contoh: "Borobudur is a famous temple in Indonesia. It is located in Magelang, Central Java."

2

Description

Bagian ini berisi deskripsi detail tentang subjek yang telah diidentifikasi. Meliputi ciri fisik, sifat, kegunaan, dan karakteristik lainnya.

Contoh: "The temple has beautiful reliefs on its walls. The architecture is very unique and amazing."

Contoh Lengkap

My Best Friend

Identification:

I have a best friend named Sarah. She is my classmate and we have been friends since elementary school. Sarah is a very special person in my life.

Description:

Sarah is sixteen years old. She is quite tall and slim. She has long black hair that she usually ties in a ponytail. Her eyes are brown and very expressive. She has a beautiful smile that can brighten anyone's day. Sarah is very friendly and always ready to help others. She is also very smart and hardworking. Her favorite subjects are Mathematics and Science. In her free time, she likes reading books and playing the piano.

Ciri Bahasa

Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menggambarkan karakteristik yang umum dan tetap.

Contoh: "She has long black hair."
"The building is very tall."

Adjectives (Kata Sifat)

Digunakan untuk memberikan deskripsi yang lebih detail tentang objek.

Contoh: beautiful, tall, friendly, smart, colorful, delicious

Action Verbs

Kata kerja yang menunjukkan aktivitas atau perbuatan.

Contoh: run, walk, sing, dance, read, write, play

Figurative Language

Simile (Perbandingan langsung)

Menggunakan kata 'like' atau 'as' untuk membandingkan

"Her smile is like sunshine"

Metaphor (Metafora)

Perbandingan tanpa kata 'like' atau 'as'

"Time is money"

Personification (Personifikasi)

Memberikan sifat manusia pada benda mati

"The wind whispers through the trees"

Hyperbole (Hiperbola)

Perbandingan yang berlebihan untuk efek dramatis

"I've told you a million times"

Contoh Descriptive Text

Borobudur Temple

Identification:
Borobudur is a famous Buddhist temple in Indonesia. It is located in Magelang, Central Java. This temple was built in the 9th century during the Sailendra dynasty.

Description:
The temple is very large and magnificent. It has nine platforms with a central dome at the top. The walls are decorated with beautiful reliefs that tell stories about Buddha's life. There are 504 Buddha statues inside the temple. The architecture is very unique and shows the greatness of ancient Javanese civilization. From the top of the temple, visitors can see the beautiful panorama of the surrounding mountains and villages.

My Favorite Teacher

Identification:
I would like to describe my favorite teacher, Mr. Smith. He is my English teacher who has taught me for two years. He is one of the most influential people in my life.

Description:
Mr. Smith is a middle-aged man with a friendly face. He is quite tall and has a medium build. His hair is starting to turn gray, but it makes him look wise and experienced. He always wears glasses and has a warm smile that makes students feel comfortable. His teaching method is very interesting. He always uses creative ways to explain difficult concepts. He is very patient and never gets angry when students ask many questions. He is not only a good teacher but also a good friend who always gives good advice.

Latihan Identifikasi

Soal 1:

Baca teks berikut dan identifikasi bagian identification dan descriptionnya:

My bedroom is my favorite place in the house. It is where I spend most of my time. My room is not very big, but it is very comfortable. The walls are painted light blue, my favorite color. There is a single bed near the window with blue and white sheets. Next to the bed is a small wooden table with a lamp and some books. On the opposite wall, there is a wardrobe and a bookshelf filled with my favorite novels. The room has a large window that lets in plenty of sunlight during the day.

Identification: "My bedroom is my favorite place in the house. It is where I spend most of my time."

Description: "My room is not very big... until the end of paragraph."

Soal 2:

Sebutkan 5 adjectives yang ada dalam teks di atas!

1. favorite, 2. big, 3. comfortable, 4. light (blue), 5. small, 6. wooden, 7. large

Soal 3:

Buatlah descriptive text pendek tentang orang yang kamu kagumi!

Tips Menulis Descriptive Text

1

Pilih Topik yang Spesifik

Pilihlah satu objek yang ingin dideskripsikan dan fokus pada detail-detailnya. Hindari terlalu banyak objek dalam satu teks.

2

Gunakan Indera Lima

Libatkan pembaca dengan menggambarkan apa yang bisa dilihat, didengar, dirasa, dicium, dan dirasakan.

3

Gunakan Kata Sifat yang Variatif

Hindari penggunaan kata sifat yang sama berulang-ulang. Gunakan sinonim untuk membuat teks lebih menarik.

4

Gunakan Figurative Language

Tambahkan simile, metaphor, atau personifikasi untuk membuat deskripsi lebih hidup dan menarik.

5

Organisir dengan Baik

Susun deskripsi secara logis, misalnya dari yang umum ke khusus, atau mengikuti urutan spasial (dari atas ke bawah, dll).

Kamus Kata Sifat

Untuk Orang

• friendly (ramah)
• cheerful (ceria)
• helpful (penolong)
• generous (dermawan)
• intelligent (cerdas)
• hardworking (rajun)
• patient (sabar)
• humorous (humoris)

Untuk Tempat

• spacious (luas)
• cozy (nyaman)
• modern (modern)
• ancient (kuno)
• beautiful (indah)
• peaceful (tenang)
• crowded (ramai)
• clean (bersih)

Untuk Benda

• colorful (berwarna-warni)
• shiny (mengkilap)
• rough (kasar)
• smooth (halus)
• heavy (berat)
• light (ringan)
• expensive (mahal)
• cheap (murah)

Kata Transisi

• First of all (Pertama-tama)

• In addition (Selain itu)

• Moreover (Terlebih lagi)

• Furthermore (Selanjutnya)

• In conclusion (Kesimpulannya)
