Descriptive Text

Mempelajari cara menggambarkan orang, tempat, dan benda dengan bahasa yang menarik

Generic Structure

Struktur umum yang digunakan dalam descriptive text

1

Identification

Bagian ini berfungsi untuk mengenalkan subjek yang akan dideskripsikan. Biasanya berisi informasi umum tentang siapa atau apa yang akan dibahas.

Contoh:

Borobudur temple is one of the most famous tourist destinations in Indonesia.

→ Mengenalkan tempat (Borobudur temple)

My best friend is someone who always supports me in every situation.

→ Mengenalkan orang (My best friend)

2

Description

Bagian ini berisi deskripsi detail tentang subjek yang telah diidentifikasi. Meliputi ciri-ciri fisik, sifat, kegiatan, dan karakteristik lainnya.

Contoh:

It has large gray body, long trunk, and big floppy ears.

→ Deskripsi fisik

She is very kind, intelligent, and always helps others.

→ Deskripsi sifat

Language Features

Ciri-ciri bahasa yang digunakan dalam descriptive text

Adjectives

Menggunakan kata sifat untuk menggambarkan ciri-ciri

Beautiful garden
Tall building
Clean room

Simple Present

Menggunakan simple present tense untuk fakta umum

She has long hair
The temple is ancient
They live in the forest

Linking Verbs

Kata kerja penghubung seperti is, am, are

She is beautiful
They are friendly
I am happy

Descriptive Text - Person

My English Teacher

Identification:
My English teacher, Mrs. Smith, is one of the most inspiring people I have ever met.

Description:
She is a middle-aged woman with long brown hair and bright blue eyes. She always wears elegant clothes and a warm smile on her face. Mrs. Smith is very patient and understanding. She explains difficult concepts in a simple and clear way. Her classes are always interesting and interactive, making students enthusiastic about learning English.

Descriptive Text - Place

Bali Island

Identification:
Bali is a beautiful island located in Indonesia that is famous for its stunning beaches and rich culture.

Description:
The island has pristine white sand beaches with crystal clear water. There are many ancient temples with intricate stone carvings. The local people are very friendly and welcoming. Bali offers delicious traditional food and vibrant traditional dances. The weather is warm and sunny throughout the year, making it a perfect destination for tourists.
