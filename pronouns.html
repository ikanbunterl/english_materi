Pronouns

Mempelajari berbagai jenis kata ganti dan penggunaannya dalam kalimat

Apa itu Pronouns?

Pronouns (kata ganti) adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menggantikan kata benda (nouns) agar tidak terlalu berulang dalam kalimat.

Personal Pronouns

Tabel Personal Pronouns

Subject Object Contoh
I me I love you. / You love me.
You you You help her. / She helps you.
He him He calls them. / They call him.
She her She likes it. / It likes her.
It it It eats food. / Food eats it.
We us We see them. / They see us.
They them They know us. / We know them.

Perbedaan Subject dan Object

  • • Subject pronouns sebagai subjek kalimat
  • • Object pronouns sebagai objek kalimat
  • • Menghindari pengulangan kata benda
  • • Membuat kalimat lebih efisien

Contoh Penggunaan

Subject Pronouns

• I am a student.

• He is my brother.

• She likes reading.

• It is raining.

• We are friends.

• They play football.

Object Pronouns

• She loves me.

• I see him every day.

• We help her with homework.

• The cat chases it.

• They invite us to the party.

• I call them later.

Pengganti Nama

Tanpa pronouns:

"John met Mary. John gave Mary a book. Mary thanked John."

Dengan pronouns:

"John met Mary. He gave her a book. She thanked him."

Possessive Pronouns

Tabel Possessive Pronouns

Personal Possessive Adjectives Possessive Pronouns Contoh
I my mine This is my book. / This book is mine.
You your yours That is your car. / That car is yours.
He his his This is his pen. / This pen is his.
She her hers That is her bag. / That bag is hers.
It its - The dog wagged its tail.
We our ours This is our house. / This house is ours.
They their theirs That is their garden. / That garden is theirs.

Perbedaan Penting

  • • Possessive adjectives diikuti kata benda
  • • Possessive pronouns berdiri sendiri
  • • Menunjukkan kepemilikan atau hubungan
  • • 'Its' tidak memiliki bentuk possessive pronoun

Contoh Penggunaan

Possessive Adjectives

• This is my book.

• That is your car.

• His name is John.

• Her hair is long.

• Its color is red.

• This is our house.

• That is their dog.

Possessive Pronouns

• This book is mine.

• That car is yours.

• The pen is his.

• The bag is hers.

• This house is ours.

• That dog is theirs.

Perbandingan

Dengan possessive adjectives:

• My car is red. / Your bike is blue.

Dengan possessive pronouns:

• The red car is mine. / The blue bike is yours.

Reflexive Pronouns

Tabel Reflexive Pronouns

Personal Reflexive Contoh
I myself I hurt myself.
You yourself You cut yourself.
He himself He blames himself.
She herself She made herself a cake.
It itself The cat cleans itself.
We ourselves We enjoyed ourselves.
They themselves They helped themselves.

Fungsi Reflexive Pronouns

  • • Menunjukkan subjek dan objek adalah orang yang sama
  • • Digunakan untuk penekanan (emphasis)
  • • Menyatakan kegiatan yang dilakukan sendiri
  • • Dibentuk dengan menambahkan -self/-selves

Contoh Penggunaan

Subjek dan Objek Sama

• I saw myself in the mirror.

• He cut himself with a knife.

• She made herself a sandwich.

• We enjoyed ourselves at the party.

• They helped themselves to food.

Untuk Penekanan

• I myself will do it.

• He himself said that.

• The president himself visited.

• We ourselves made this cake.

Dengan 'By'

• I did it by myself. (saya sendiri)

• He lives by himself. (sendirian)

• She solved the problem by herself.

Relative Pronouns

Tabel Relative Pronouns

Who

Untuk orang (subject)

The man who lives next door is friendly.

Whom

Untuk orang (object) - formal

The woman whom you met is my aunt.

Which

Untuk benda atau hewan

The book which is on the table is mine.

That

Untuk orang atau benda

The car that I bought is red.

Whose

Menunjukkan kepemilikan

The boy whose bike was stolen is crying.

Fungsi Relative Pronouns

  • • Menghubungkan dua kalimat menjadi satu
  • • Merujuk pada kata benda sebelumnya (antecedent)
  • • Membuat teks lebih efisien dan mengalir
  • • Dapat diganti dengan 'that' dalam banyak kasus

Contoh Penggunaan

Who (Subject)

• The teacher who teaches English is nice.

• The man who lives next door is friendly.

• Students who study hard will succeed.

Whom (Object)

• The person whom I called is my boss.

• The girl whom you met is my sister.

• The man whom we helped was grateful.

Which (Benda/Hewan)

• The book which is on the table is mine.

• The car which he bought is expensive.

• The dog which barks loudly is scary.

That (Alternatif)

• The man that lives here is rich.

• The book that I read was interesting.

• The movie that we watched was boring.

Interrogative Pronouns

Daftar Interrogative Pronouns

What

Untuk benda atau informasi

What is your name?

Who

Untuk orang (subject)

Who is coming to the party?

Whom

Untuk orang (object) - formal

Whom did you meet?

Which

Untuk pilihan terbatas

Which book do you prefer?

Whose

Menanyakan kepemilikan

Whose bag is this?

Fungsi Interrogative Pronouns

  • • Digunakan untuk membuat pertanyaan
  • • Menanyakan informasi tentang orang, benda, atau kepemilikan
  • • Selalu diikuti oleh verb
  • • Berbeda dengan interrogative adjectives

Contoh Penggunaan

What (Benda/Informasi)

• What is your favorite color?

• What time is it?

• What do you want to eat?

• What happened yesterday?

Who (Orang - Subject)

• Who is knocking at the door?

• Who wants to go first?

• Who called you last night?

• Who is your best friend?

Which (Pilihan)

• Which do you prefer, tea or coffee?

• Which is your car?

• Which way should we go?

• Which book are you reading?

Whose (Kepemilikan)

• Whose is this jacket?

• Whose book are you reading?

• Whose turn is it?

• Whose phone is ringing?

Ringkasan Pronouns

Jenis Fungsi Contoh Keterangan
Personal Menggantikan orang I, you, he, she, it, we, they Subject & object forms
Possessive Menunjukkan kepemilikan my, mine, your, yours Adjectives & pronouns
Reflexive Subjek = objek myself, yourself, himself -self/-selves
Relative Menghubungkan kalimat who, which, that, whose Relative clauses
Interrogative Membuat pertanyaan what, who, which, whose Wh- questions
