Pronouns (Kata Ganti)

Mengenal berbagai jenis kata ganti dalam Bahasa Inggris

Apa itu Pronoun?

Pronoun atau kata ganti adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menggantikan nama orang, benda, atau tempat agar tidak mengulang kata yang sama berkali-kali dalam kalimat.

Personal Pronouns

Kata ganti untuk orang

First Person

Orang pertama (saya/kita)

I

I am a student

Me

She helps me

We

We are friends

Us

They see us

My/Mine

This is my book

Second Person

Orang kedua (kamu/anda)

You

You are kind

You

I help you

You

You are students

You

We meet you

Your/Yours

That is your car

Third Person

Orang ketiga (dia/mereka)

He

He is tall

She

She is pretty

It

It is big

Him

I see him

Her

We help her

It

They want it

They

They are happy

Them

We call them

Jenis-jenis Pronoun

Macam-macam kata ganti dan penggunaannya

Subject Pronouns

Kata ganti yang digunakan sebagai subjek kalimat

I You He She It We You They

Contoh: She is beautiful

Object Pronouns

Kata ganti yang digunakan sebagai objek kalimat

Me You Him Her It Us You Them

Contoh: I love her

Reflexive Pronouns

Kata ganti yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan tindakan kembali ke subjek

Myself Yourself Himself Herself Itself Ourselves Yourselves Themselves

Contoh: She hurt herself

Possessive Pronouns

Kata ganti yang menunjukkan kepemilikan

Mine Yours His Hers Its Ours Yours Theirs

Contoh: This book is mine

Demonstrative Pronouns

Kata ganti yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan benda

This That These Those

Contoh: This is my pen

Interrogative Pronouns

Kata ganti yang digunakan untuk membuat pertanyaan

Who Whom Whose Which What

Contoh: Who is she?

Word of Adjective Series

Urutan kata sifat dalam bahasa Inggris

Urutan yang Benar:

Opinion → Size → Age → Shape → Color → Origin → Material → Purpose

Penjelasan:

Opinion: beautiful, ugly, nice, amazing

Size: big, small, tall, short

Age: old, young, new, ancient

Shape: round, square, triangular

Color: red, blue, green, yellow

Contoh:

Benar:

A beautiful small old round red Indonesian wooden dining table

Salah:

A red beautiful Indonesian old small round wooden dining table

Latihan:

Coba buat kalimat dengan 3-4 kata sifat!
